What can we do?

Ours is the age of environmental breakdown brought about by human destruction of our world. The mainstream media may fixate on the role of the individuals but only a fundamental transformation of our current system can reverse the current trends. While individuals alone may not be able to make drastic emissions cuts, personal action can help pressure policymakers and businesses into a reformed system.
